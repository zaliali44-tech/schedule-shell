
# 📅 Weekly Staffing Scenario Planner

A lightweight, browser-based tool for operations and workforce planning teams to estimate headcount requirements and generate weekly shift schedules — instantly, with no sign-up or installation needed.

🔗 **[Live Demo →](https://zaliali44-tech.github.io/fix-hc-schedule/)**

-----

## 🧩 What It Does

The Weekly Staffing Scenario Planner helps you answer the question:

> *“How many agents do I need, and how should I schedule them?”*

You input your daily headcount (HC) demand for each day of the week, and the tool:

- Compares **5-day, 6-day, and 7-day** work patterns side by side
- Calculates the **minimum HC** required for each pattern
- Applies a **planning buffer** to arrive at a final HC target
- Generates a **complete weekly schedule** with working days and off days per agent
- Shows a **coverage summary** so you can spot any gaps
- Exports results as downloadable files

-----

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Enter daily HC requirements** for Monday through Sunday
1. **Choose a week-off pattern** (Best Fit, Continuous, or Split)
1. **Select a planning buffer** (0–3 extra agents)
1. Click **Calculate Scenario** to compare work patterns
1. Select your preferred work pattern (5-day, 6-day, or 7-day)
1. Click **Generate Schedule** to produce the weekly roster
1. **Export** the schedule, coverage table, or scenario summary

-----

## 🎯 Who Is This For?

- **Operations managers** planning shift-based or contact centre teams
- **Workforce planners** building weekly schedules from demand forecasts
- **Team leads** who need a quick headcount estimate without complex WFM software
