Weekly upload format should be:
Interval, Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun
Example:
09:00,20,20,20,10,20,28,20
09:30,24,22,24,12,24,30,24

For 5 or 6 operating days, the tool treats the remaining days as closed in this order: 5 days = Mon-Fri open, 6 days = Mon-Sat open, 7 days = Mon-Sun open.
